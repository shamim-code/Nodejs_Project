This is a crud operation project with node js. But without Database. You can add,update,delete and get all products.


Here is the links of API-

  • To get all products list: http://localhost:5050/getall
  • To create new product: http://localhost:5050/create
  • To update a specific product by id: http://localhost:5050/update/id
  • To delete a specific product by id: http://localhost:5050/delete/id
